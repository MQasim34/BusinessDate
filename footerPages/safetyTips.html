SAFETY TIPS

1

Meeting in public

1. Get their name, number, don’t be in a rush to meet. If you decide to meet then do so in the day, in a well lite public environment

2. Never meet someone the first time in their residence or bring them to your residence. Instead meet in coffee shop or restaurant where there is a lot of people around Transportation

3. Don’t let your date pick you up on the first date or even the second date

4. Make sure you come to meeting with your own transportation, car, uber, bus etc. don’t rely on that person for transportation on especially on first few meetings

5. If you drive your own vehicle, make sure you park in a well-lit and populated area, watch them leave before you if you park further in the parking lot

6. advantages of having your own transportation is that it will be easy for you to make it out of the date quickly if you need to. Also have money to pay your taxi or uber ready to use if needed

7. During the meeting avoid giving away your personal information

Tell a friend/family member

1. Inform your friend, relative about your meeting, where you are going to meet, whom you are meeting and what time your will be meeting. You can even make a phone call in front of the person you are meeting just to make them know someone knows your whereabout

4. Engage your match on the MBSM platform and don’t rush to get into the texting, messaging, emails etc. And don’t let anyone rush you to do these things

5. Keep your login information for MBSM platform private, don’t share with others and be careful when signing on in a public place like he library or coffee shops

Stay Sobber
